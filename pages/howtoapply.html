How To Apply To UAES

How To Apply To UAES

Like other universities in Nigeria, admission into graduate programmes are handled by JAMB via the UTME examination. Candidates who wish to study in Imo state university of agriculture and environmental sciences must meet all requirements set by JAMB and also those of the department/course he/she wishes to study.

All candidates who chose Imo state university of agriculture and environmental sciences(UAES) as their first or second choice and even those who did not choose it but now wish to study in UAES must after passing the JAMB examination (with the required cut off point) visit the UAES portal and apply for their chosen course of study.


Application Steps

  1. Visit UAES portal and click on Apply Now
  2. Accurately fill the application form and submit
  3. On submitting the form, you will see an invoice for application fee payment, you have a choice to instantly pay online using your ATM cards or print the invoice and go to any commercial bank and make the payment
  4. On the invoice page, there is a button for you to print your application summary, do not forget to print your application summary page
  5. After a successful payment(either online or offline), you will get an email alert from UAES acknowledging your application and payment.
  6. Keep your application summary form and do come with it to the school if and when you eventually get admitted.
  7. When you are admitted, you will get an email alert from the school telling how to proceed with other processes. alternatively, from time to time, you can check your admission status on the same page where you just applied by clicking on "check status" and entering your application number. (the application number should have been sent to you the moment you paid your application fee)

About UAES

The University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences (UAES) Umuagwo, is a public University established by the act of Imo State Parliament Law No. 4 of 2019. The establishment of this University was a direct response of the Government of Imo State to the burning need for more credible tertiary Institutions needed to address the yearnings of the teeming youths of Imo State and the broader South-Eastern Nigeria desiring for qualitative third level education.
